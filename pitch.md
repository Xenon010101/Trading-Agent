SLIDE 5 - THE AI BRAIN
---
# Groq LLaMA 70B Decision Engine

Every 10 minutes InsiderEdge:

1. Fetches live data for BTC, ETH, SOL
2. Calculates RSI + MACD + Trend scores
3. Sends data to Groq LLaMA 70B
4. AI returns: action + confidence + reasoning
5. Only trades if confidence above 60%

**Example Decision:**
Action: BUY
Confidence: 75%
Reason: MACD bullish + uptrend + positive momentum.
RSI at 58 — not overbought. Low volatility reduces risk.
